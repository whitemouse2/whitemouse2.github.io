White Mouse II

White Mouse II is a privately owned twin screw motor yacht (TSMY) originally built in 1963 by Philips of Dartmouth. She is a double skinned mahogany planked vessel which underwent major renovation and refit in the 1990s. She is powered by twin 105hp Perkins diesel engines and has a 14kw Onan Generator and Vosper Thorneycroft Stabilisers. She had an electronics update in 2008 with the latest RayMarine and Furuno equipment installed including Chart Plotter, Radar, DSC VHF radio and AIS Transducer.

White Mouse II was acquired by her current owner in October 2009.

This website is for friends of the owner to find out more about her history and specification.
